# Presidio Insurance — Q1 2026 Marketing Campaign Customer Feedback Summary

**Document owner:** Marketing Operations  
**Source:** Google Docs — Customer Insights Hub  
**Period:** January 1 – March 31, 2026

## Campaign overview

Q1 campaigns focused on:

1. **"Protect What Matters"** — homeowners bundle (TV + digital)
2. **"Drive Confident"** — auto telematics launch
3. **"Small Business Shield"** — SMB commercial lines

## Aggregated feedback themes

### Positive (n = 1,842 responses)

- **Clarity of messaging:** 68% said the "Protect What Matters" tagline clearly explained bundle savings.
- **Mobile quote flow:** 61% rated the redesigned quote experience 4–5 stars.
- **Agent follow-up:** NPS for human agent callbacks after digital quotes rose **12 points** vs Q4.

### Negative (n = 412 responses)

- **Telematics privacy concerns:** 34% of auto campaign respondents asked how driving data is stored and shared.
- **Email frequency:** 22% unsubscribed citing too many promotional emails in February.
- **Price transparency:** SMB campaign feedback cited confusion about deductibles vs premium in digital ads.

## Verbatim highlights

> "The bundle calculator finally made sense — I switched both home and auto." — Customer #A-1192

> "I like the app, but I want a plain-English FAQ on what telematics tracks." — Customer #B-884

> "Too many emails after I got a quote. One reminder is enough." — Customer #C-331

## Recommended actions (Marketing + Compliance)

1. Publish a telematics data FAQ linked from all "Drive Confident" assets.
2. Cap promotional email sequences at **3 touches** per quote abandonment journey.
3. Add deductible callouts to SMB landing page hero modules.

## Cross-functional notes

- Claims team reported **no spike** in call volume tied to Q1 campaigns.
- Compliance flagged one social post draft for implied "full coverage" language — revised before publish.
