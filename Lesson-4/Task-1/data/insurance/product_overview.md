# Presidio Insurance — Product Overview (Internal)

**Classification:** Internal — Sales & Service enablement

## Lines of business

Presidio offers personal and commercial insurance across:

- Homeowners & renters
- Auto (including usage-based / telematics)
- Small business commercial package (BOP)
- Specialty lines (marine, high-value personal property)

## Underwriting principles

- Risk-based pricing using approved actuarial models
- Telematics discounts capped at **25%** pending state filings
- AI-assisted document intake is **human-reviewed** before binding

## Customer service SLAs

| Channel | Target first response |
| --- | --- |
| Phone | 60 seconds |
| Email | 4 business hours |
| Chat | 90 seconds |

## Regulatory footprint

Licensed in **42 US states**. Material rate and form changes require state filing 30 days before effective date unless expedited approval applies.
