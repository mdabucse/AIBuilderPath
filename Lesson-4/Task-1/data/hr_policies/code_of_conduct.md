# Presidio — Employee Code of Conduct

## Our values

Presidio employees act with integrity, respect, and accountability when serving policyholders, partners, and colleagues.

## Conflicts of interest

Employees must disclose financial interests in insurance carriers, brokers, or vendors that could influence business decisions.

## Harassment & discrimination

Presidio provides equal employment opportunity regardless of race, gender, religion, age, disability, or veteran status. Report concerns to HR or the ethics hotline.

## Confidentiality

Non-public information about Presidio, its customers, or its partners must not be shared outside the company without authorization.

## Social media

Employees may not represent personal opinions as official Presidio statements. Marketing campaigns require Compliance pre-approval when referencing product guarantees.

## Disciplinary action

Violations may result in coaching, suspension, or termination depending on severity.
