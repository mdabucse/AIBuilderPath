# Presidio — Remote & Hybrid Work Policy

**Effective date:** March 15, 2025  
**Owner:** People Operations

## Eligibility

Full-time employees in eligible roles may work remotely up to **3 days per week** after completing 90 days of employment. Client-facing insurance adjusters follow site-specific schedules documented by their manager.

## Equipment & security

- Company-issued laptop required for all remote work
- VPN must be active when accessing internal systems
- Personal devices may not store customer or claims data

## Performance expectations

Remote employees are measured on outcomes, not hours online. Managers conduct monthly 1:1s and quarterly performance reviews using the same criteria as in-office staff.

## Hiring trend (internal benchmark)

As of Q1 2026, Presidio has **42 open requisitions** across:

- Engineering & AI: 14 roles (+18% YoY)
- Claims & underwriting: 12 roles (+6% YoY)
- Sales & marketing: 9 roles (-4% YoY)
- Operations: 7 roles (+2% YoY)

People Operations publishes an updated hiring dashboard on the first Monday of each month.
