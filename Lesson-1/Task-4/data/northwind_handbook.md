# Northwind Robotics — Employee Handbook

> Sample knowledge base for the RAG chatbot demo. Northwind Robotics is a
> fictional company; all details below are made up for testing retrieval.

## About the Company

Northwind Robotics was founded in 2016 in Portland, Oregon. The company builds
warehouse automation robots and the software that coordinates them. As of 2025
it employs 480 people across three offices: Portland (headquarters), Austin,
and Berlin.

The company's mission is "to make physical work safer and more predictable
through dependable robots." Northwind's CEO is Dr. Amara Osei, and the CTO is
Liang Chen.

## Working Hours and Remote Work

Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM in the employee's local time
zone, with a flexible core-hours window of 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM during which all
team members are expected to be reachable.

Northwind operates a hybrid model. Employees are expected in the office at
least **two days per week**. Fully remote arrangements require director approval
and are reviewed every six months.

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

- Full-time employees accrue **20 days** of paid vacation per year.
- PTO accrues monthly and unused days roll over, capped at **30 days** total.
- Employees also receive **10 paid public holidays** and **8 sick days** per year.
- Parental leave is **16 weeks** fully paid for all new parents.

To request time off, submit a request in the Workday portal at least two weeks
in advance for any absence longer than three consecutive days.

## Health and Benefits

Northwind covers 100% of the medical and dental premium for employees and 75%
for dependents. The company contributes a **6% match** to the 401(k) retirement
plan, vesting after two years of service.

Every employee receives an annual **$1,200 learning stipend** for courses,
books, and conferences, plus a one-time **$500 home-office setup allowance**.

## Equipment and IT

New engineers are issued a 16-inch MacBook Pro by default; other roles may
choose between macOS and Windows laptops. Hardware refresh happens every three
years. IT support is reachable at help@northwind.example and responds within
one business day for non-urgent issues.

## Code of Conduct

All employees must complete annual security and anti-harassment training.
Confidential customer data must never be copied to personal devices. Violations
should be reported to People Operations or through the anonymous ethics line.

## Performance Reviews

Performance reviews happen twice a year, in **June and December**. Reviews use a
combination of self-assessment, peer feedback, and manager evaluation. Promotion
decisions are made during the December cycle.
