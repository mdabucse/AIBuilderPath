Reimbursement Process:
To file a reimbursement, use the Expensify portal. Submit all receipts within 30 days of the expense.

Budget Reports:
Last month's budget report can be found in the shared Google Drive under Finance -> Reports.

Payroll:
Payroll is processed on the 15th and the last day of every month. If those fall on a weekend, it is processed on the preceding Friday.
